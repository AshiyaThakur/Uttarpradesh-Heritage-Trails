Kanpur


Kanpur, formerly anglicised as Cawnpore, is a large industrial city located in the central-western part of the state of Uttar Pradesh, India.

Prayagraj


Prayagraj is one of the oldest cities in India. It is crowned in ancient scriptures as 'Prayag' or 'Teertharaj' and is considered the holiest of pilgrimage centres of India.

Varanasi


Varanasi, also called Beneras,Banaras, or Kashi, city, southeastern Uttar Pradesh state, northern India. It is located on the left bank of the Ganges (Ganga) River and is one of the seven sacred cities of Hinduism.

Lucknow


Lucknow is the capital city of Uttar Pradesh. Courtly manners, beautiful gardens, poetry, music, and fine cuisine patronized by the Persian-loving Shia Nawabs of the city are well known amongst Indians of South Asian culture and history.

Agra


Agra is best known for the Taj Mahal, Taj Mahal is considered to be the world's best example of Mughal architecture.Mughal emperor Shah Jahān built it for his favourite wife, Mumtāz Maḥal.

Mathura


In Hinduism, Mathura is the birthplace of Krishna, which is located at the Krishna Janmasthan Temple Complex. It is one of the Sapta Puri, the seven cities considered holy by Hindus, also is called Mokshyadayni Tirth.

Jhansi


Nestled between the Betwa and Pahunj rivers, the historic city of Jhansi is synonymous with its courageous queen, Rani Lakshmibai, who valiantly fought against the British forces during the First War of Indian Independence in 1857.

Ayodhya


An ancient town, Ayodhya is regarded as one of the seven sacred cities of the Hindus, revered because of its association in the great Indian epic poem Ramayana with the birth of Rama and with the rule of his father, Dasharatha.
